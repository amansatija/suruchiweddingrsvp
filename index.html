About Michael

Michael is amazing. When I first met him, I knew he was the rock i needed in my life and with every passing day he reminds me of just how wonderful he is! What I really find unique about him is his unique blend of talents, his charming personality and splendid interests. He’s never afraid of the challenges life throws at him and is always excited about trying new things. What’s more, he’s genuinely a loving person. Michael can be doing anything from leading a company to playing with kids and be equally happy. And this rare quality makes him a joy to be around.I know I couldn’t have found a better match for me.

About Suruchi

After waiting for 14 years I finally found the love of my life. My soulmate. I knew it the moment I saw Suruchi, that my search had come to an end. Suruchi is a girl with a bright and beautiful personality who dreams of a life filled with love, excitement and adventure. She thinks with her heart, loves from her heart and gives from her heart. So when it came to falling in love, she didn’t think about any boundaries, changing her country or switching her job. She kept her heart first and that alone was her guide. I guess it guided her well.
